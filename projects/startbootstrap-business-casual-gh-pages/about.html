About Truth Be Told About Enemy Lines

This Story orginated from the creation of the character Katerina, Kat for short, and her journey through the world of supernatural beings created by the government.

She discovers her whole life was a lie and fights back against the people who hunted her. She was told that she needs to keep running, but when the person who tells her to run has haunted her nightmares for years, does she follow, or turn back?

Main Characters

Demetri Blackburn Agent at the CIA Black Ops Branch for supernatural abilities. Age : 30

Katerina Walker Teenager who is cursed with the gift of sight, but now tortured to predicit acts of terroism. Age : 17

Niklaus Blackburn Demetri's younger brother, who had the gene to be subjected to the experiemnts of supernatural abilites. After realizing the truth behind the project, he broke out of the facility and hasnt been seen since... or has he? Age : 25
